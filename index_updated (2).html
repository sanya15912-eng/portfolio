ABOUT ME

Hey there,
I'm Sanya

B.Sc. Animation & VFX Student | Multi-Disciplinary Designer

I am a 20-year-old creator driven by a simple obsession: making things look visually unforgettable. Currently pursuing my B.Sc. in Animation and VFX, I spend my time blending the rules of motion with the fundamentals of static design.

When I'm not rendering frames or tweaking visual effects, I'm building brand identities from scratch.
